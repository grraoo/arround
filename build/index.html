What is ARROUND

Thanks to augmented reality technology and Big Data, ARROUND brings our experience of the Internet into the physical world. With the help of user and advertiser generated "rounds", users can find the best products, the most interesting people and the freshest recommendations wherever they are.

The ARROUND Platform is a fun and dynamic way to communicate and experience interactive AR content, and a powerful new mobile advertising channel

The project’s strategic partner is “36.6” — Russia’s largest pharmacy network

15% of the network is owned by Walgreens Alliance, the world's biggest pharmacy chain

Project Structure

Mobile Application

Основной способ взаимодействия с миром ARROUND в среде дополненной реальности

Server

Служит для хранения и обработки раундов, распределения и подгрузки данных по локациям, а также для хранения профилей пользователей и другой внутренней информации, необходимой для существования платформы

Portal for Advertisers

Ресурс для создания рекламных раундов и организации рекламных каампаний на площадке ARROUND

Blockchain Server

Гарантирует собственность средств в кошельках пользователей платформы, обеспечивает соблюдение интеллектуальных прав на контент внутри платформы, а также регулирует права на долгосрочную аренду участков AR-пространства внутри ARROUND

Use Case: Retail Shop

ARROUND allows you to target ads to the platform’s users. For example, if the advertiser places an advertising "round" on the signboard of his shop, when it enters the camera view of ARROUND, the user sees the ad. The advertising message will depend on the social demographic. For example, if the advertiser is a pharmacy, a young mother will see an ad for diapers, while a middle-aged man might see vitamins

Roadmap

2016

Initial idea to create a communication platform based on augmented reality
Optimization of the project’s business concept
Presentation of idea and feedback

2017

Technical specification of the project
First major partner, A.V.E. which runs the biggest pharmacy network in Russia
Investment into the development of MVP

2018

Release mobile app on iOS and Android
Запуск рекламных кампаний с новыми партнерами 36.6 и ТЦ Атриум
Release of the alpha version, seed the the platform with content and open the doors to targeted users

2019

Port ARROUND to Augmented Reality Glasses ODG and Microsoft HoloLens
Launch advertising campaigns with large pharmacy chains across Europe
Engage advertising agencies on the platform
Develop self-service module for ad agencies to build their own AR content

2020

The first auction in the secondary market for the sale of advertising space inside the platform
Launch of SDK, attracting third-party developers to the platform
Expansion to new markets: China, Japan, Korea, Europe
Launch of a pilot project with a partner in the US

FAQ

  • What are ARROUND tokens and what are they used for?

    The ARR Token is a universal currency of the ARROUND ecosystem.

    For the convenience of users and advertisers, prices in the ARROUND system will be denominated in fiat currencies, but the token will be used to complete all purchases of digital goods and services in the ecosystem.

    The ARR token will be released on the basis of the ERC20 smart contract on the Ethereum blockchain network, and will be traded on cryptocurrency exchanges.

    The maximum number of ARR tokens is 3,000,000,000.

  • When will the token offering take place?

    Pre-sale: 02.27.2018 - 05.01.2018

    Token Sale: 05.15.2018 - 07.15.2018

  • Do you have the MVP already?

    MVP in the final testing phase.

  • Will ARROUND tokens be available on exchanges after the ICO?

    We are negotiating with Etherdelta, Yobit, and Binance HitBTC. We are going to pass the listing immediately after the ICO.

  • Will you have a bounty or referral campaign?

    Yes. Details will be announced shortly. Subscribe to our newsletter to be the first in line.

  • How do I participate in the Pre-Sale/Crowd-sale?

    To take part in the token offering, you should register on our website and send your crypto to the token offering address. Your tokens will be put to your account in our system automatically, and after the crowdsale they will be sent to your sending address. If you want to contribute crypto other than ETH you still need to give your Ethereum account address.

  • What is the ARR cap?

    The hardcap will be announced after the Pre-sale.

  • Are there minimum and maximum purchases?

    No, but the will be 5% extra bonus on purchases worth more than US $10,000.

  • Which currencies do you accept?

    We accept Ethereum, Bitcoin, Litecoin, Bitcoin Cash and Dash. If you want to contribute crypto other than ETH you still need an Ethereum account.
